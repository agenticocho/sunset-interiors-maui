Common Questions

Q: How much does popcorn ceiling removal cost on Maui?

A: Popcorn ceiling removal typically runs $1–$6 per square foot, with most Maui homes falling between $500 and $2,500 depending on square footage and ceiling height. Call Kevin for an exact on-site quote.

Q: What is a smooth-wall (level-5) finish?

A: A level-5 smooth-wall finish is the highest-grade drywall finish — a full skim coat of joint compound applied over the entire surface, then sanded glass-smooth so wall light doesn't reveal texture or seams. It's the premium look for modern Maui homes.
